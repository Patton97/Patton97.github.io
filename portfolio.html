Projects I've worked on

Caravan Carnage Club

Started as a 'jam game' for GlobalGameJam, this is by far the most fun project I've worked on to date. Myself and other members of Northumbria's Game Dev Society took the 2019 theme "What home means to you" and...well we ran with it. Within the 48 hours of the jam, we focused on creating the spiritual successor to Snake that aboslutely nobody asked for. Crash into enemy cars to steal their caravans, collect powerups to go fast or get drunk, see what hi-score you can get without before time runs out!
